About the Artist

Neha Chawla

Based in sunny San Diego, Neha creates artwork that captures emotion, color, and story. Under her brand Neso by Neha, she brings a distinctive style to every piece — whether it's a vibrant abstract, a heartfelt portrait, or a custom creation for a special occasion.

Each artwork is made with intention and care. Neha works across multiple mediums and loves collaborating closely with clients on custom orders that hold personal meaning.
